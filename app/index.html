about me

I was raised in Estherwood, Louisiana, a rural village not far off of I-10. I went to Remington College in Lafayette, Louisiana at night and worked full time during the day . I love Cajun food and culture. I currently reside in Austin, Texas.

When I am not working, I am enjoying things I'm passionate about, such as: art, nature, travelling, literature, spontaneous adventures, and philanthropy . I am an active member of Women Who Code in Austin. I often frequent coffee shops and can be found along the banks of the Colorado River watching the currents flow by while writing poetry and sketching. Live music, food and art festivals easily grab my attention.

I have been in web and software development over 12 years and I specialize in frontend technologies. Single Page Applications (SPAs) and Responsive Web Design(RWD) are my niches. My work is calculated and handcrafted component by component.
